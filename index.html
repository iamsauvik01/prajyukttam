About

Prajyukktam 2023, our university's annual tech fest, is set to be an exciting blend of innovation and knowledge. With cutting-edge tech showcases, seminars, and thrilling competitions, it's a platform for students to shine. Prajyukktam 2023 promises to ignite curiosity and creativity, leaving a lasting impact on our university's tech landscape. Join us on this extraordinary journey from the 28th to the 30th of September!

Prajyukktam 2023 is not just about innovation and knowledge; it's also your chance to win big! Get ready for exhilarating competitions that offer substantial prize money. We have something for every tech enthusiast, from the thrilling Data Fest, a two-day hackathon competition that will test your coding and analytical skills, to the Bridge Making challenge that will put your engineering prowess to the test. And if you're up for a challenge that's all about speed and precision, join the Race to Solve the Circuit the fastest. With impressive cash rewards and recognition on the line, these competitions are bound to fuel your competitive spirit and provide an unforgettable experience at Prajyukktam 2023. Don't miss out on this opportunity to showcase your talents and take home some impressive prizes! Join us on this extraordinary journey into the world of technology and innovation.
